About the Editor

Welcome to my blog!

My name is Ben, and I live in central Massachusetts and aspire to be a transportation planner in the future. I enjoy riding transit when I can and starting this blog is an exciting new project!

My only experience with anything related to this was when I wrote a guest post on the popular blog "Miles in Transit", and my guest post was on the Grafton MBTA station, which I have also wrote about on this blog. I'm also a photographer.

I hope you enjoy this blog!
